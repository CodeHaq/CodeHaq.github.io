Recipe #2 - ChocoMarshBites

  • Time: 50 mins
  • Servings: 12
Ingredients Quantity
Light brown sugar 3/4 cup
Granulated sugar 1/4 cup
Non-alcoholic vanilla extract 2 tsp
Salt 1/2 tsp
All-purpose flour 2 cups
Cornstarch 2 tsp
Baking soda 1 tsp
Egg 1
Unsalted butter 3/4 cup
Semi-sweet chocolate chips 1 packet
Marshmallows! 1 packet

Step 1: In a large bowl, use a wooden spoon, a rubber spatula or a hand-held mixer with paddle attachments to beat the butter.

Step 2: Add the brown sugar and sugar to the butter and mix until the colour turns light brownish.

Step 3: Add the egg and the vanilla and beat until combined and creamy. About 2 minutes on medium speed with the hand-held mixer.

Step 4: In a separate bowl, combine the dry ingredients like all-purpose flour, salt, baking soda and cornstarch and beat with an egg beater until properly mixed.

Step 6: Add chocolate chips and, on low speed, mix them until they are well combined and not sticking to the bowl that much.

Step 7: Cover the bowl with plastic wrap or aluminum foil and cool in the fridge for at least 1 hour.

Step 8: leave at room temperature for 10 minutes. Preheat oven to 177°C or 350°F and aline 2 baking sheets with parchment paper or silicone baking mats. Set aside.

Step 9: Heat marshmallows on a non-stick pan or microwave for 10 seconds until soft and chewy. Set aside

Step 10: The dough will be slightly crumbly but work it with your hands and make balls using 1.5 tablespoons each.

Step 11: Open the cookies, place the slightly heated marshmallows in them, close them up and place them on the baking sheets.

Step 12: Bake for 12 minutes until barely golden brown around the edges and cool them for 5 minutes.
